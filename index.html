Relaynet will help circumvent Internet blackouts

Relaynet-compatible apps will use the Internet when it's available, but they'll switch to a backup medium when the Internet has been cut off.

  • No special hardware required
  • Protection against eavesdroppers
  • Free and open source

About Relaynet

Relaynet was designed and prototyped at the University of Oxford, and its development is now led by Relaycorp. We won the first place at the Mozilla Builders Open Lab, and we've been implementing the network thanks to funding from the Open Technology Fund and the International Republican Institute.
