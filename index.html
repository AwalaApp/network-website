Relaynet will help circumvent complete Internet blackouts

Relaynet-compatible apps will use the Internet when it's available, but they'll switch to a backup medium when the Internet has been cut off.

All data is end-to-end encrypted Users remain anonymous at all times Use your existing phone or computer Free, open source and decentralized

Background

Relaynet is a decentralized technology with technical specifications publicly available and open source implementations. The project won the first place at the Mozilla Builders Open Lab (summer 2020 cohort) and its alpha testing on Android is due to start in February 2021 thanks to funding from the Open Technology Fund and the International Republican Institute.
